In June 2026, the author was appointed as the **Lead Principal Investigator** of the **"Science Intelligence (AI4S) Secure Service Middleware" sub-project** under China's **"New Generation AI" National Major Project**, with total funding of **¥10 million**.

The sub-project focuses on building the security infrastructure and foundational capabilities for Science Intelligence (AI4S), aiming to provide a unified security-as-a-service middleware for scientific computing, drug discovery, and industry simulation, ensuring security evaluation, trustworthiness enhancement, and compliance governance across the entire AI4S lifecycle.
